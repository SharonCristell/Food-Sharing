Food Sharing: A Spring Boot Web App

This application with the concept of front-end and back-end development was created by using SpringBoot, Thymeleaf, Bootstrap, Spring Security, and also the implementation of the database connection comes with PostgreSQL. This food-sharing tracker was done by Sharon Cristell Quispe Carhuapoma as a Final project for the Server Programming course. The ultimate purpose of this application is to create a database of items such as food and non-food household products that could be shared or needed by a user in order to keep track and unlock the value of items wasted in the home and local community. Therefore, the application contains two demos segmented by users: user/user (with the role of USER) and administrators: admin/admin (with the role of ADMIN). The idea of the app stems from the fact that most of my friends, neighbours or in general any kind of people want to share the excess of food going spare.
